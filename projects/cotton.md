Netflix Recommendation System is a machine learning project where a model was trained to identify the user's taste based on his past ratings fo the movies and that was used to recommend new movies to the user.

<hr>

<ul>
  <li>Used Matrix Factorization using Gradient Descent and Clustering using K Means to build two different recommendation systems and compare their performances.</li>
  <li>Top 10 movies were suggested for the users based on the ratings given to other movies by the user.</li>
  <li>RMSE and Silhouette score was used to measure the individual algorithm performances and the top movie suggestions from both the algorithms were compared.</li>
</ul>
